# EURU System Structure

EURU is organized into modular layers, each representing a core function of the system.

---

## 📁 00_GOVERNANCA / 01_GOVERNANCA
Responsible for system governance, rules, validation and decision control.

---

## 📁 02_BINANCE_SETUP
Exchange integration and environment configuration.

---

## 📁 03_ARQUITETURA
System design, structural definitions and operational logic.

---

## 📁 04_AGENTES
Agent definitions (Dream Team), responsible for analysis and signal validation.

---

## 📁 05_PROMPTS
Prompt engineering layer used by agents.

---

## 📁 06_RISCO_E_EXECUCAO
Risk management and execution rules.

---

## 📁 07_OPERACAO
Operational flows and execution routines.

---

## 📁 08_DADOS_E_JOURNAL
Data storage, logs and decision journaling.

---

## 📁 09_LOGS_E_INCIDENTES
System monitoring, logs and incident tracking.

---

## 📁 10_AUTOMACOES
Automation processes and scheduled tasks.

---

## 📁 11_CONFIG_PLACEHOLDERS
Configuration templates and environment placeholders.

---

## 📁 99_ARQUIVO_E_NOTAS
Archive and supporting notes.

---

## 🧠 Design Philosophy

EURU follows a modular architecture where each layer has a specific responsibility.

This ensures:

- Scalability
- Maintainability
- Clear separation of concerns
